JRC and OSM Mapping:
The keywords to classify assets into residential, industrial, commercial are listed below. 
Each asset which equals or includes parts of the keyword is classified as such. 
Some keywords have the same meaning but are written in different forms e.g. Apartment (capital "A" and apartment). Different variations in spelling are included but not explicitly listed here.

commercial
